On a Quest for Impact

I am a dedicated researcher and educator with a robust academic foundation in Geography, Climate Change, and Sustainable Development. As an Erasmus Scholar currently pursuing a master's degree in Digital Earth at Paris-Lodron University in Salzburg, Austria, I specialize in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), spatial analysis, and advanced geospatial tools. With hands-on experience in utilizing Copernicus data and technologies, I am passionate about applying my technical and analytical expertise to drive initiatives focused on climate resilience and sustainable development. My goal is to contribute innovative solutions that bridge the gap between geospatial research and actionable strategies for a more sustainable future.
