Leveraging Advanced Spatial Analysis, 3D Mapping, and Geospatial Decision-Making

I have gained valuable experience working on a variety of projects using ArcGIS Pro, which have enhanced my skills in 3D mapping, spatial analysis, and geospatial data visualization. These projects showcase my ability to utilize advanced GIS tools and methodologies to address real-world challenges in urban planning, environmental management, and disaster risk reduction. Below are some of the key projects I have successfully completed using ArcGIS Pro:

  • 3D Urban Infrastructure Modeling:Created 3D models of urban infrastructure, including buildings, roads, and public utilities, to support city planning and development initiatives.
  • Multi-Temporal Land Use and Land Cover (LULC) Change Analysis: Analyzed changes in LULC over time using multi-date satellite imagery, supporting environmental impact assessments and land management efforts.
  • Disaster Risk Assessment and Mitigation Mapping: Identified high-risk areas for natural hazards like floods, landslides, and earthquakes by analyzing topographic, hydrological, and geological data.
  • Site Suitability Analysis for Renewable Energy Projects:Conducted spatial analysis to identify the most suitable locations for renewable energy sites, such as solar farms and wind turbines, using multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA).
  • Hydrological Analysis and Watershed Delineation:Delineated watersheds and analyzed drainage patterns using Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) to support water resource management and flood risk assessment.
