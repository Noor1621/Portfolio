    Geospatial Expertise

    Showcasing Expertise in Spatial Analysis, Mapping, and Geospatial Decision-Making

    I have gained extensive experience working on a variety of geospatial projects using ArcGIS Desktop. My expertise spans multiple domains, including urban planning, environmental management, and network optimization. Through these projects, I have developed strong skills in spatial data analysis, cartographic visualization, and geoprocessing techniques. Each project reflects my ability to analyze complex datasets, create insightful maps, and support evidence-based decision-making. Here are some of the key projects I have successfully completed using ArcGIS Desktop:

    • Geohazard Risk Mapping.
    • Fiber-to-Home Networking.
    • Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) Mapping
    • Site Suitability Analysis
    • Watershed and Hydrological Mapping

    Fiber-to-Home Networking: Designed and optimized fiber-optic network layouts to ensure efficient and cost-effective connectivity for residential areas. This project involved route planning, distance calculations, and cost analysis.

    Population Density Mapping: Analyzed and visualized population distribution patterns to support urban planning, resource allocation, and demographic studies. This project utilized census data, geospatial datasets, and thematic mapping techniques.

    Land Use/Land Cover (LULC) Mapping: Conducted LULC mapping to classify and monitor changes in land use, aiding in environmental impact assessments and sustainable development planning.

    Geohazard Risk Mapping: Identified and visualized areas prone to natural hazards, such as landslides and floods, to support disaster preparedness and risk reduction efforts.

    Transportation Network Analysis: Mapped and analyzed transportation routes, traffic flow, and connectivity for better route optimization and infrastructure planning.

    Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) Mapping: Supported environmental assessments by mapping ecological zones, protected areas, and critical habitats to evaluate the impact of development projects.

    Site Suitability Analysis: Used spatial analysis tools to identify optimal locations for specific projects, such as infrastructure development or renewable energy sites, by analyzing multi-criteria datasets.

    Watershed and Hydrological Mapping: Delineated watershed boundaries and analyzed drainage networks for water resource management and flood risk assessment.
