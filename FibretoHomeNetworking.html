Optimizing High-Speed Fiber Connectivity Through Advanced GIS Mapping

Fiber-to-Home (FTTH) Network Project, where we designed and optimized a high-speed fiber-optic network to deliver seamless internet connectivity directly to residential homes. Using ArcGIS Desktop and carried out route planning, cost estimation, and network design to ensure an efficient and cost-effective deployment.

My responsibilities included mapping existing infrastructure, identifying optimal routes for fiber cable installation, and minimizing construction costs by selecting the shortest and most feasible paths. I utilized spatial analysis tools to calculate distances, analyze terrain, and identify potential obstacles such as water bodies, highways, and other barriers.

This project enhanced my technical proficiency in network design and demonstrated the practical application of GIS in telecommunications infrastructure development, ultimately supporting the rollout of faster and more accessible internet services for communities.
